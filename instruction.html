Nico Nail

Wearing Instruction

How to wear it?

Step one, use a small wooden stick to push up the dead skin around the nail edges.
Step two, gently polish the nail surface.
Step three, clean the surface with an alcohol swab.
Step four, choose a jelly gel suitable for the size of your nails.
Step five, apply the jelly gel to the nail surface, ensuring it is evenly applied without any air bubbles, using a tool if needed.
The final step, affix the nail at a 45-degree angle to the nail surface, pressing firmly.
